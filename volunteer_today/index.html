Sound Athletics is always looking for volunteers to assist with our program. We are looking for passionate people to provide direction and encouragement for our athletes. No experience is required; your presence alone can make a difference!

Volunteer Activities

Bowling (Fall) – Help athletes find a bowling ball, shoes, and a lane. Help the athletes determine when it is their turn to bowl and to track their scores.

Basketball (Winter) – Help athletes with dribbling, passing, and shooting drills. Help athletes learning the flow of the game.

Track (Spring) – Help athletes with warm up and stretching. Help athletes with lane positioning. Help athletes transition from track to field events. Help coaching staff record times and distances.

How to Volunteer

  1. Check out our Calendar to see if our practices fit your schedule.
  2. Complete the Volunteer Registration Form and the WSP Background Request Form. Then deliver both forms in person to Angela, team Secretary, at our practice. If you are interested in becoming a Unified Peer Partner, then also complete the Unified Partner Release Form.
  3. Come to practice and have fun!

For more information on volunteering, please email us at soundathleticspierce@gmail.com or call Ann, Team Manager, at (253)306-9239.
